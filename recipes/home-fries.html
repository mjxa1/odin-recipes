Homestyles Fries

Description

Fries, a great staple of American cuisine. They compliment just about any dish and are delicous pretty much anytime of day or night. What aren't so great though, are the saltiness and greasiness that usually come from ordering fries when out and about. Also, why spend money on something that can be made just as delicous in your own home, while also being a bit lighter to digest as well. Keep reading to learn how to accomplish this incredible feat!

Ingredients

  • 1 potato
  • seasoning(s) of choice
  • olive oil
  • an air fryer

Steps

  1. Rinse and dry potato
  2. chop potato in half
  3. cut each half in half
  4. cut each quarter into strips of desired thicccness
  5. place strips on plate and season to personal preference
  6. pour 1 tablespoon of olive oil onto seasoned potato strips
  7. hand mix until all strips are evenly coated with seasoning and olive oil
  8. microwave strips for 3 minutes
  9. preheat airfryer @ 390f
  10. place warmed potato strips into airfryer
  11. let cook for 15 minutes or until desired crispyness
  12. place on paper towel to cool & dry
  13. Enjoy!
