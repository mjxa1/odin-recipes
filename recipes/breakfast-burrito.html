Breakfast Burrito

Description

Quick, simple, delicious: I couldn't think of a better way to describe a go-to breakfast for the busy people out in the world. A breakfast burrito has all of those qualities while also being incredibly portable for those on the go. Keep reading to learn how to make one today!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • meat of choice (i.e. bacon, sausage, etc.)
  • cheese of choice (i.e. cheddar, parmesan, gouda, etc.)
  • 1 tortilla
  • 1 slice of butter

Steps

  1. Prepare meat in whatever way you desire
  2. scramble egg, seasoned however you prefer
  3. once meat & egg are cooked, warm up tortilla in microwave for 15 seconds
  4. place your chosen cheese(s) on bottom third of tortilla
  5. place egg on top of cheese
  6. chop up meat into bite-sized chunks
  7. place chopped meat on top off egg
  8. Optional, but not really ;): Add more cheese on top of meat
  9. fold left and right sides of tortilla in
  10. roll tortilla from bottom up
  11. heat skillet to medium heat
  12. butter heated skillet
  13. grill "not closed" side of burrito in skillet for 4 minutes
  14. flip and grill other side for 4 more minutes
  15. Let cool and enjoy!
